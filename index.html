Rice (Paddy)

💧 Watering Needs: Maintain a consistent water level of 2-5 cm in the paddy. Not based on times per day.

🌱 Fertilizer Needs: High in Nitrogen (Urea), Phosphorus (P), and Potassium (K). Apply in split doses.

🕒 Time to Harvest: Approximately 90-120 days from sowing, depending on the variety.

Tomato

💧 Watering Needs: Water 1-2 times a day, ensuring the soil is moist but not waterlogged. Avoid wetting the leaves.

🌱 Fertilizer Needs: Balanced NPK fertilizer initially, with higher Potassium during the fruiting stage.

🕒 Time to Harvest: Roughly 60-85 days after transplanting the seedlings.

Wheat

💧 Watering Needs: Requires irrigation every 20-25 days. Critical stages are crown root initiation and flowering.

🌱 Fertilizer Needs: A balanced mix of Nitrogen, Phosphorus, and Potassium is crucial for good yield.

🕒 Time to Harvest: Typically ready for harvest in 100-120 days.

📜 Government Schemes & Alerts (Odisha)

Access information on central and state government schemes relevant to your farming profile in Odisha.

  • KALIA (Krushak Assistance for Livelihood and Income Augmentation)

    Focus: Direct financial support. Provides payments to small farmers, marginal farmers, and landless agricultural households for farm activities.

    Benefit: Financial assistance of ₹12,500 for landless families and income support for cultivators for seeds, fertilizers, etc.

  • Odisha Millets Mission (OMM)

    Focus: Crop Diversification & Nutrition. Promotes the cultivation, processing, and consumption of millets (like Ragi) to improve farmer income and nutrition.

    Assistance: Provides incentives, technical support, and market linkages for millet farmers.

  • Balaram Yojana

    Focus: Credit for Landless Farmers. Aims to provide agricultural credit to landless farmers through Joint Liability Groups (JLGs).

    Benefit: Facilitates loans of up to ₹1.60 lakh per group, enabling access to credit without land collateral.
